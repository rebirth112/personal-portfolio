Who Am I?

I'm Jerry, a graduate of Simon Fraser University in BC, Canada. I studied for a BBA and primarily held administrative, managerial, and B2B sales roles for 6 years.

My interest in programming began when I figured out how to use Python to automate my administrative tasks, with useful libraries such as Selenium, and OS.

I then discovered the world of web development, and the impact that it can have on businesses large and small.

With over 1.5 billion active websites today, I decided to learn everything I could about web development, both as a potential career path and a source of secondary income through freelance work.

In addition, I possess a Graduate Certificate in Business Analytics from the British Columbia Institute of Technology, which focused heavily on data analysis and visualization using Python, SQL, and Power BI.
